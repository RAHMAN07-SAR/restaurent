Yes, we maintain vegetarian dishes and can adapt many items for gluten-free diners. Please inform us of any dietary needs.

Is parking available?

We provide valet service on weekends and validated parking on weekdays after 6 pm.

Use the contact form below and select “Private Dining” in the message. Our events team will respond within 24 hours.
