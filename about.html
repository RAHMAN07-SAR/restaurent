About Us

Our story, mission, and values

Founded on a love for the sea and a commitment to sustainability, Redes Restaurant partners with responsible fisheries and local producers. Our mission is simple: serve exceptional seafood that respects the ocean and delights our guests.

  • Sustainable sourcing & seasonal menus
  • Open kitchen & chef’s counter experiences
  • Private dining & bespoke events
Meet the Team

Our Journey

2010 - The Beginning

Redes Restaurant opened its doors with a simple idea: bring the freshest seafood to the heart of the city.

2015 - Recognition

We were awarded “Best Seafood Restaurant” and expanded with a chef’s table experience.

2022 - Today

Continuing our mission of sustainable dining while offering innovative cuisine in a warm setting.
